About

I don't speak Japanese. Yet I fall for the artist Miyuki Nakajima pretty hard. As a fan, I find it very difficult to talk to non-Japanese speaking friends about the artistry of the great Japanese singer/songwriter. One of the issues being the lack of English translation of her works. This is the beginning of this website.

If you have any edits about the translations made available on this website, or any other questions, issues, complaints, please do not hesitate to drop my an Email at mymealprepplanner@gmail.com.

Thank you for your visit!
